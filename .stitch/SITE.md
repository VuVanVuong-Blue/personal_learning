# Personal Learning Platform - Site Architecture
## 1. Vision
A sleek, modern educational platform featuring AI-generated custom learning roadmaps. The design is strictly Minimalist with an Indigo brand color, focusing on typography, whitespace, and a distraction-free "Zen" reading experience punctuated by Apple-like UI sleekness.

## 2. Global Components
- **Sidebar (Left):** Minimalist navigation, sticky, separated from the right by a 1px border (`border-r border-slate-200`). White or off-white.
- **Header:** Often integrated into the top of the right pane or spanning the top.
- **Main Area (Right):** The primary view, which uses off-white background and white cards.
